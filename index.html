Web Development

This video goes into some detail about all the tools necessary to develop modern websites. After watching it, I'm glad I'm learning from the ground up. I am currently taking this course because I would like to design websites for a living. A few years ago, I was on a solo trip to Tamarindo, Costa Rica. I happen to be a huge coffee drinker, so on my trip I stopped in alot of little coffe shops in Tamarindo. In these coffee shops, I ran into tons of Americans who were living abroad, spending their days clicking away on laptops, when they weren't in the water surfing or snorkeling. It seemed like a much slower and better way to live, the locals call it "Tico Time". What they all had in common were computer skills that gave them the freedom to work from anywhere with a wifi connection. When I got home I couldn't stop thinking about them. So I'm taking this class to see if this old dog can learn some new tricks.
